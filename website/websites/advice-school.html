Quick study plan

Create weekly goals, use 25–30 minute focused sessions and review notes actively. Small, consistent steps beat last-minute cramming.

Study smarter — professional tips

Plan with time-blocking

Use 25–30 minute focused study blocks with 5–10 minute breaks (Pomodoro). Review difficult topics first and keep short summary notes.

Group and peer study

Study with peers to explain concepts to each other — teaching is a powerful learning tool. Keep sessions short and focused on one topic.
