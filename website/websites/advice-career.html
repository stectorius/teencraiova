Ideas for building experience early: volunteering, short courses, internships, and how to write a simple CV.

Start small

Look for short free online courses and local volunteer roles. Note your projects and activities in a simple CV format.

Professional advice for getting started

Build practical skills

Choose 1–2 small, project-based courses (web, design, languages). Keep a short portfolio (projects, screenshots) — this helps more than long lists of courses.

Romania resources: ANOFM (job programs), eJobs.ro

Find opportunities

Look for internships, volunteer positions and local workshops — these can lead to real contacts and references.

Romania resources: European Youth Portal, Erasmus+ programs and local NGOs.

CV & interview basics

Keep CVs short (one page), list relevant projects and clear contact info. Practice short answers to common interview prompts: "Tell me about a project" or "What did you learn?"

Try free templates and quick mock-interviews with a friend or teacher.

Further reading & training

  • ANOFM — training and employment programs (Romania)
  • eJobs.ro — job listings and internships
  • Coursera / free courses and Khan Academy for skills practice
