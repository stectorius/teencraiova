Advice & Resources

Practical tips, trusted resources and local support to help you with school, career choices, mental health and everyday life as a teen in Craiova.

School & Study

Boost your grades with study plans, time management tips and exam techniques. Try weekly study goals, use the Pomodoro method, and form small study groups with classmates.

  • Make a simple timetable and stick to short, focused study blocks.
  • Ask teachers for past papers and feedback — they can guide your revision.
  • Use online resources for explanations and practice (Khan Academy, YouTube tutorials).

Mental Health & Well-being

Looking after your mental health is just as important as studying. Small habits make a big difference.

  • Keep a simple routine: sleep, food, movement, and short breaks during study.
  • Talk to someone you trust if you feel stressed or anxious — a friend, parent, teacher or a counsellor.
  • If you need professional help, contact local health services or reach out via our contact email.

Career & Skills

Explore career ideas and build useful skills early. Volunteering, short courses and internships help you understand what you enjoy.

  • Try short online courses (programming, design, languages) to find your interests.
  • Prepare a simple CV listing school projects, clubs and volunteer work.
  • Attend local workshops and meetups — check our Events page for upcoming opportunities.

Relationships & Communication

Healthy relationships are built on respect and clear communication. Set boundaries and listen actively.

  • Practice saying how you feel using "I" statements ("I feel... when...").
  • If you’re experiencing bullying or harassment, tell a trusted adult and document what happened.

Online Safety

Protect your privacy and well-being online. Think before you post and use privacy settings on social platforms.

  • Use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication where possible.
  • Don’t share personal or financial details in chats or comments.
  • If something makes you uncomfortable online, block and report the user, and tell an adult.
